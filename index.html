Theme Features

Overview: a galaxy & constellation-inspired theme with a container frame, posts, 12 constellations to choose from, 8 custom links, and a sidebar image.

Post container frame:

  • background image: tiled/repeated
  • thin borders: goes all the way around the container frame. 4 tiny boxes at the corners for decoration
  • thickness: customizable; enter a numerical value

Posts:

  • customizable: width, padding, photoset gutter width, permalink padding, bottom post margin, border option
  • options: photo fade & monochrome images

Sidebar:

  • colors: correspond to the ones in your posts
  • constellation: zodiac constellations; 12 to choose from
  • custom links: up to 8; look best with 6 to 8. try to keep them short
  • sidebar image: 64px by 64px
  • description: looks best with 4 lines. optional description scrollbar (upon overflow)
  • 4 default links: please do NOT remove the theme credit
  • pagination in sidebar: optional. turn off and pagination will appear at bottom of posts

Tumblr controls:

  • hover option: invisible to visible
  • dark tumblr controls option: turns to black

Animated stars in background:

  • optional. if you have a slower/older computer, I recommend turning this option OFF (or else you may experience lag) (as I also do)

Coding credits:

  • PXU photoset tutorial, lightbox tutorial, video resizing script, like & reblog button tutorial by shythemes
  • inline images resizing script by gukthemes
  • “SaturnIcons” icon font by saturnthms
  • “rotating stars” pen by Lucas Bebber

Image credits:

  • the sidebar icon in this preview: “Light” by Shilin
  • background image is by davehugs

Please follow my Terms Of Use when using this theme, and feel free to send me a message if you encounter any problems! I will try and help you to the best of my ability. Enjoy!
